CLINICAL FEATURE ENGINEERING AUDIT
==================================

1. FEATURES
-----------
- HOMA_IR: glucose and insulin interaction used as a rough insulin resistance proxy.
- BMI_Age_Ratio: BMI normalized by age.
- Glucose_BMI_Interaction: combined glucose and BMI signal.
- Metabolic_Risk_Score: train-fitted normalized score across glucose, BMI, insulin, and blood pressure.
- Pregnancy_Risk: pregnancies scaled by age.
- Age groups: fixed age buckets for non-linear age effects.

2. LEAKAGE CHECK
----------------
- Train/test split happens before preprocessing is fitted.
- Imputer, feature engineering, scaler, and classifier are fitted only on the training split.
- Metabolic_Risk_Score stores train-set min/max values during fit.
- `tests/test_data_leakage.py` checks that one patient's score is unchanged when another patient appears in the same inference batch.

3. MODEL STACK
--------------
- Base model: XGBoost classifier.
- Calibration: CalibratedClassifierCV with sigmoid calibration.
- Tuning: seeded Optuna search on transformed training folds.

4. LATEST FULL TRAINING SNAPSHOT
--------------------------------
- ROC-AUC: 0.7768
- F1 score: 0.8014
- Recall/sensitivity: 0.8692
- Precision/PPV: 0.7434
- Brier score: 0.1804

5. LIMITS
---------
- The data is synthetic.
- Feature names are clinically inspired, but that does not validate them clinically.
- SHAP output is useful for model inspection, not causal interpretation.
